# My Favorite Cake Recipe :cake:

## Ingredients

* Flour
* Sugar
* Butter
* Eggs
* Oil
* Vanilla
* Milk
* Baking Powder

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Grease and flour pan.
3. Mix sugar, butter until creamy
4. Add eggs one at a time.
5. Stir in vanilla
6. Sift flour and baking powder.
7. Add oil to wet incredients
8. Add flour in three batches
9. Bake for 35-40 minutes
